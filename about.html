About Me

Hey there! I’m Chelsie!

I’m an Atlanta native and an aspiring HCI/UX designer majoring in Information Science at Cornell University. With a heart for serving and problem-solving, I aspire to use my love for technology to empower, encourage, satisfy, serve, and improve the quality of life for others!

During the summer of 2020, I volunteered to design and build the website for a contracting company, Trinity Property LLC, where I was able to enhance my ideation, prototyping, design, and UX persuasion skills.

When I am not designing and creating, I enjoy English horseback riding, streaming YouTube videos, and jamming to music!
