Chick-fil-A Internship

During the summer of 2021, I served as a Business Analyst Intern at the Chick-fil-A Support Center. I worked closely alongside my intern coach, Stacey Jones, and Jessica Bass on the Enterprise Menu Data Management (EMDM) team helping to manage the data that supports Chick-fil-A’s digital menus.

I engaged in three projects, protected by NDA , where I gained valuable experience in:

  • designing low to medium fidelity wireframes and prototypes for users’ needs gathered by conducting multiple user interviews
  • communicating requirements between software engineers and non-technical parties
  • entering and linking data in web applications to its supporting databases
