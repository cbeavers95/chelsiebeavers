Chick-fil-A Support Center Internship

Timeline: June 2021 - August 2021

During the summer of 2021, I served as a Business Analyst Intern at Chick-fil-A Support Center. I worked closely alongside my intern coach, Stacey Jones, and Jessica Bass on the Enterprise Menu Data Management (EMDM) team helping to manage data that supports Chick-fil-A’s digital menus. I engaged in three projects, protected by NDA , where I gained valuable experience in designing low to medium fidelity wireframes and prototypes for users’ needs gathered by conducting multiple user interviews, communicating requirements between software engineers and non-technical parties, and entering and linking data in web applications to its supporting databases.
